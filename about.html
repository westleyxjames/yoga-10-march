About Yoga With Us

Building a global community of wellness seekers

Our Mission

Empowering Lives Through Yoga

At Yoga With Us, we believe that yoga is for everyone. Our mission is to make the transformative practice of yoga accessible, welcoming, and empowering for people of all ages, backgrounds, and fitness levels.

We're committed to creating a supportive community where individuals can explore their potential, overcome limitations, and discover the profound benefits of mindful movement and meditation.

Our Story

How We Started

Yoga With Us was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate yoga practitioners who wanted to share the life-changing benefits of yoga with the world. What started as a small studio with just three instructors has grown into a thriving online community serving over 10,000 students globally.

Our founders recognized that traditional yoga studios weren't accessible to everyone—whether due to location, schedule constraints, or intimidation factors. They envisioned a platform that would break down these barriers and bring expert instruction, community support, and authentic yoga teachings to anyone with an internet connection.

Today, we're proud to offer hundreds of classes taught by certified instructors, covering everything from gentle beginner flows to advanced vinyasa sequences. Our community continues to grow, united by a shared commitment to wellness, mindfulness, and personal transformation.
