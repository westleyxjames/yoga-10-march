FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Don't see what you're looking for? Contact us!

How do I get started?

Simply click on any of our free yoga classes to begin. For full access to our premium content, you can sign up for a free trial.

Are the classes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We offer classes for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Each class is clearly labeled with its difficulty level.

What equipment do I need?

All you need is a yoga mat and comfortable clothing. Some classes may suggest optional props like blocks or straps, but they're not required.

How quickly will I see results?

Many students report feeling benefits after just one class! With consistent practice (3-4 times per week), you'll notice improvements in flexibility, strength, and mental clarity within a few weeks.
